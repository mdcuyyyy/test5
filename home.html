About Kek & Hutan

Kek & Hutan is a well-known cafe in Kota Bharu, Kelantan. It is popular for its cakes, especially Sarawak layer cakes and also serves other dishes such as mee kolok and Sarawak laksa. Many visitors like the cafe for its cozy, aesthetic atmosphere, making it a nice place to relax, enjoy desserts and spend time with friends or family.
